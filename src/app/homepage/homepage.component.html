About Us

The Resilient Utility Coalition (RUC) was created with the objective of advancing utility infrastructure resilience efforts.

Partners from the utilities, professional associations, industry, and academia are working together to operationalize policy and practices in assessing and adapting utility operations to address the increasing number of challenges faced by utilities worldwide.

Mission

By operationalizing resilience through interdisciplinary and integrated planning we are improving water quality, public health, the efficient use of resources, and ensuring responsible investments in our utilities.

Vision

We are resilient utilities in a One Water coalition

OWA/RUC Partnership

The Resilient Utility Coalition (RUC) and the One Water Academy (OWA) share various commonalitites in their core mission of advancing training, professional development, mentoring, and knowledge sharing within the water, wastewater, and stormwater utility industries.

For the purposes of work being performed in the South Florida region, OWA and RUC will work jointly and collaboratively on all initiatives so that we maximize the outputs from our volunteers.
