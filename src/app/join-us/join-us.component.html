We invite you to join the Resilient Utility Coalition!

The Resilient Utility Coalition seeks to advance utility infrastructure resiliency efforts in South Florida and provide essential value to its members and partners.

The Resilient Utility Coalition provides leadership helping utility operations address the potential effects of climate change. We seek to enhance the usefulness of climate science by developing adaptation strategies and improving water management decision-making in the face of climate uncertainty.
